1. Who We Are

Versant ("we," "us," or "our") is a dating application that connects people through voice and conversation before revealing photos. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency.

Contact us at: privacy@versantapp.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Versant:

  • Account information — name, nickname, email address, age, city, state
  • Profile information — gender, preferences, occupation, education level
  • Compatibility quiz answers — used only for matching, never shown to other users
  • Photos — stored securely and hidden until mutual reveal
  • Voice notes — stored temporarily for 30 days
  • Messages — stored for 30 days from creation
  • Device information — device type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with compatible people based on your quiz answers and preferences
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Keep the app safe through moderation and safety systems
  • Improve the app and user experience
  • Send you important notifications about your account
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Your Photos

Your photos are stored securely and are never shown to any other user until both you and your match have mutually agreed to reveal. We do not use your photos for any purpose other than displaying them after a mutual reveal.

5. Your Quiz Answers

Your compatibility quiz answers are used exclusively by our matching algorithm to find compatible matches. They are never displayed to other users, never sold to third parties, and never used for advertising purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Messages and voice notes — deleted after 30 days
  • Reported content — retained for up to 7 years for legal purposes
  • Safety reports and moderation logs — retained permanently
  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • IP address logs — retained for 2 years

7. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with advertisers. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect the safety of our users or the public
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

8. Safety and Moderation

Versant has a safety system to protect users. When you report a message, the content is saved permanently for legal purposes. Users who receive multiple reports may be suspended or permanently banned. We review all reports within 24 hours.

For safety concerns contact us at: safety@versantapp.com

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights contact us at privacy@versantapp.com

10. Children's Privacy

Versant is strictly for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18 we will immediately terminate their account and delete their data.

11. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information including encryption in transit and at rest. However no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong unique password for your account.

12. Third Party Services

Versant uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase — secure database and storage
  • Expo — mobile app platform
  • Apple — iOS app distribution

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Your continued use of Versant after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
